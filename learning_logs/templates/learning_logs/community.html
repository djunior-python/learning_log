Our Principles

Welcome to the Learning Log community. This platform is designed for learners to create, share, and track their learning progress.

✅ Allowed

  • Sharing educational content
  • Using the site for learning and collaboration
❌ Not Allowed

⚖️ Consequences

Violations of these rules may result in account suspension or permanent ban.
